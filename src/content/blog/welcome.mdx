I've been meaning to start writing for years. I never did, because there was always something more urgent and because writing about my own work felt strange when so many smarter people were already writing about theirs.

That changed for me recently. After spending most of 2024 and 2025 building internal AI tooling at [GotPhoto](https://www.fotograf.de/), I noticed something: the questions worth answering aren't always the ones that look impressive. Often they're small, practical, and lived-in. The kind of thing you'd tell a coworker over coffee, not write a thread about.

So this blog is going to be that — a place for the small, practical, lived-in stuff. Mostly engineering. Probably some opinions about tooling, agents, and the way our jobs are quietly changing. Occasionally something off-topic.

If you find anything here useful, that's the whole point. If you find something you disagree with, even better — drop me a line on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sulman-azhar) or [email](mailto:sulmanazhar10@gmail.com).

More soon.
